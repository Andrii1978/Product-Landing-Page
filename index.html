Welcome to Wooden, the best manufacturer of lumber that meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. We offer a wide range of sawn timber in a variety of sizes and wood species to suit all needs.

Our company prides itself on providing high quality lumber and services to all types of customers, from individual consumers to large construction companies. We work closely with our customers to ensure that they receive the best possible service and lumber to suit their needs.
